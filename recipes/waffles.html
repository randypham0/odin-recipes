Classic Waffles

Description

Breakfast is said to be one of the most important meals of the day. Treat yourself in the morning and try these waffles. Another simple yet tasty recipe, its golden exterior, crispy texture, and sweet syrup, you will want to come back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking poweder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
